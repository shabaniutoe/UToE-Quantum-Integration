Structural Constraints on Quantum Integration Dynamics

Majid Shabani
Independent Researcher
December 2025

## Abstract

Quantum systems exhibit integration dynamics across entanglement,
measurement, and interaction with the environment. While quantum mechanics
provides precise local rules, the global accumulation of quantum correlations
and information is subject to structural constraints.

This work examines whether selected quantum integration measures display
bounded, monotonic saturation behavior consistent with logistic-style
integration dynamics. Rather than asserting universality, the analysis
identifies specific regimes where bounded integration is mathematically
compatible and clear counterexamples where it is not.

The results establish a conservative boundary for integration-based
descriptions of quantum systems without modifying established quantum theory.
